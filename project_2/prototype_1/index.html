NEIL HARBISSON: THE WORLD'S FIRST
CYBORG
TUESDAY 02.16—2016 AT THE NEW SCHOOL
FROM 7:00PM TOM 9:00PM
Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics (STEAM) at Parsons presents a discussion with Neil Harbisson, a Catalan-raised, British-born contemporary artist and cyborg activist. As a cofounder of the Cyborg Foundation, Neil is the first person to fully recognized by a governmental body as a "Cyborg". Neil will be exploring his unique experience through conversation. STEAM at Parsons School of Design leverages internal design and art expertise to further foster collaborative relationsihps witing the NYC community in an effort to advocate for the value of arts in education.
